Design of the web
for the Czech Vogue

MY ROLE

  • UX design and supervision
  • Google Analytics

THE CLIENT

  • Vogue CZ-SK
  • Done under Symbio Agency

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • From the first brief to launch in 4 months

Design and research

My first task was to collect requirements from the client, do research and create the first specification (wireframe in Axure) for the web structure. I then reviewed it with developers to see what was possible within the deadline. I processed more detailed wireframes and I ran a workshop with the client in which the whole structure was evaluated.

Team collaboration

Wireframes were just the beginning of my work. When dealing with an ad-hoc and constantly changing project like this one, we had to comunicate constantly. I collaborated closely with the whole team on a daily basis including regular meetings with the graphic designer to make every detail perfect; daily communication with the client to answer all his/her questions; processing of new requirements; meetings with the development team to make sure they are informed of the latest changes; and checking the functionality of what had been implemented.

Another challenge was that for all of the modulat components, we had to think of how will they look like in CMS, so the editors will be able to use it efectively.

Launch

We successfully and miraculously launched the website on time.

The website exceeded all KPIs in terms of visits, magazine subscriptions, newsletter subscriptions, and returning visitors.
